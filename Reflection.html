HUMCOM Reflection

This semester in Human-Computer Interaction was a very interesting experience. I enjoyed learning how technology is designed not just to work, but to be easy and enjoyable for people to use.

Doing activities like usability testing helped me see how small design choices affect users. Some exercises were challenging, but they taught me to think creatively and pay attention to details.

Overall, HCI made me more aware of the user experience. I want to use what I learned to create designs that are simple, useful, and user-friendly in the future.
