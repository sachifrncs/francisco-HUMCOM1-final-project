About GTX-SCHZI

Founded in 1900 by visionary entrepreneur Chenwazu, GTX-SCHZI began as a humble workshop crafting high-quality bicycles for the local community. Chenwazu’s passion for innovation and commitment to craftsmanship quickly earned the company a reputation for durability and performance.

Throughout the 20th century, GTX-SCHZI expanded its product line and embraced cutting-edge technology, becoming a leader in the bike industry. From classic road bikes to modern electric models, GTX-SCHZI has consistently delivered products that meet the evolving needs of cyclists worldwide.

Today, GTX-SCHZI is proudly operated by Sachi, Chenwazu’s great-grandchild, who continues the family legacy with the same dedication to quality and innovation. Under Sachi’s leadership, the company combines tradition with modern design, focusing on sustainability and community engagement.

GTX-SCHZI remains committed to empowering riders of all levels and continues to push the boundaries of cycling excellence.

Our Vision

At GTX-SCHZI, we envision a world where cycling is not just a means of transportation, but a lifestyle that connects people to nature, community, and themselves. We strive to lead the industry by combining over a century of craftsmanship with cutting-edge innovation, creating bicycles that are sustainable, reliable, and accessible to riders of all ages and skill levels.

Our goal is to inspire a global movement toward healthier living and environmental responsibility by providing products that empower every rider to explore with confidence and joy. We are committed to continuous improvement, pushing the boundaries of design and technology while honoring the legacy and values instilled by our founder, Chenwazu.

Through collaboration, creativity, and care, GTX-SCHZI aims to build a future where every journey on two wheels contributes to a cleaner planet and stronger communities.
